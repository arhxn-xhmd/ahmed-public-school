8. Co - curricular Activities

"Co-curricular activities: where skills meet passion, shaping minds beyond the classroom."
Discover a world of opportunities at Ahmed Public School! Here's a glimpse of the exciting co-curricular activities we offer to inspire, engage, and empower every student.

Swimming : At Ahmed Public School, swimming is more than just a sport—it's a life skill that builds physical fitness, boosts confidence, and teaches perseverance, all while making a splash in the pool!

Cycling : At Ahmed Public School, cycling is more than just a ride—it's a journey of endurance, freedom, and exploration. It builds physical stamina, sharpens focus, and instills a sense of adventure, all while promoting a healthy and eco-friendly lifestyle.

Skating : At Ahmed Public School, skating is a blend of balance, agility, and creativity. It builds physical coordination, boosts confidence, and teaches perseverance, adding a fun and thrilling dimension to student life.

Horse Riding : At Ahmed Public School, horse riding is a blend of discipline, focus, and connection with nature. It builds physical strength, enhances emotional bonding, and teaches responsibility, offering students a unique and enriching experience.

Dance and Music: "At Ahmed Public School, dance and music come together to inspire creativity, self-expression, and teamwork. They build confidence, enhance cultural awareness, and provide a joyful outlet for students to explore their artistic talents.
