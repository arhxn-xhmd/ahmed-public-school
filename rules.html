7. School Rules & Policies

  1. Regular attendance is mandatory, and absences require a valid reason or medical certificate.
  2. Students must arrive 10 minutes before assembly; latecomers may not be allowed without justification
  3. Leave applications must be submitted in advance for planned absences.
  4. The prescribed school uniform must be worn neatly and with pride at all times.
  5. Sports uniforms are only allowed on designated days or during physical education classes.
  6. Accessories, makeup, and colored hair are strictly prohibited.
  7. Students must maintain discipline and respect teachers, staff, and peers at all times.
  8. Bullying, harassment, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated.
  9. Use of mobile phones or electronic devices is prohibited during school hours.
  10. Students must complete all assignments and homework on time.
  11. Damage to school property must be reported immediately and may require compensation.
  12. Participation in school events, assemblies, and activities is compulsory unless exempted.
  13. Parents and visitors must follow school protocols and obtain permission before meeting students or staff.
  14. The school promotes a clean and green environment; littering is strictly prohibited.
  15. Students are expected to uphold the school's values of integrity, respect, and excellence at all times.
  16. Students must carry their school ID cards daily and present them when requested.
  17. Cheating or plagiarism in exams or assignments will result in strict disciplinary action.
  18. Students are not allowed to leave the school premises during school hours without prior permission.
  19. All students must participate in morning assemblies and follow the instructions given.
  20. Library books must be returned on time and kept in good condition; fines apply for late returns or damage.
  21. Use of inappropriate language or gestures is strictly prohibited.
