10. Fee Payment




















Payment Instructions

  1. Enter the student's First Name and Last Name (optional).
  2. Provide the Grade (Class & Section).
  3. Fill in the Student's ID (contains letters and numbers).
  4. Provide the Admission Number (XXXXX format).
  5. Enter the Bank Account Number for payment processing.
  6. Specify the Amount to be paid (in dollars).
  7. Double-check all the details you've entered to ensure accuracy.
  8. Click the Pay button to complete the payment.
  9. A confirmation message will appear once the payment is successful.
  10. After payment, click the Print button to save or print the payment receipt.
