9. Sports and Games

"Sports and games teach teamwork, resilience, and leadership, shaping not just athletes, but future champions."
Explore the exciting world of sports and games at Ahmed Public School, where every student can discover their passion, build skills, and embrace the spirit of teamwork and competition.

Basketball : At Ahmed Public School, basketball is a dynamic blend of agility, strategy, and teamwork. It builds physical fitness, sharpens focus, and fosters collaboration, offering students an exciting way to stay active and competitive.

Football : Football is a thrilling mix of strategy, teamwork, and endurance. It builds physical strength, enhances coordination, and teaches discipline, providing students with an exciting platform to grow and excel.

Cricket : Cricket is a perfect blend of strategy, precision, and teamwork. It builds physical endurance, sharpens focus, and teaches sportsmanship, offering students a platform to excel and enjoy the spirit of the game.

Badminton : Badminton is a fast-paced mix of agility, precision, and focus. It builds physical fitness, enhances reflexes, and teaches strategic thinking, providing students with a fun and competitive way to stay active.

Table Tennis: Table tennis is a thrilling combination of speed, coordination, and quick thinking. It builds reflexes, sharpens focus, and fosters concentration, offering students an engaging and energetic way to develop their skills.
